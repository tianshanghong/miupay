Start with a single recipient address. Settlement webhooks let you unlock content, APIs, or services the moment funds arrive.

µ Vision

µ 愿景

µ stands for micro. We build for people priced out by card fees, cross-border friction, and KYC walls. Small merchants, indie builders, and global creators deserve a payment rail that actually sees them.

µ 代表 micro。我们为那些被高额卡费、跨境成本与 KYC 门槛挡在门外的 小微个体与个人开发者而造。小生意、独立产品与出海创作者 都值得拥有真正看见他们的支付通道。
